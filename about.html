EXPLORE-TEMPLE

Sri Sri Uma ramalingeswara swamy & subramanyeswara swamy temple is 160 years historical and one of the famous temple in rangampeta mandal.This temple is located in Rangampeta in East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh. This temple is 26 kms from Rajahmundry and 37 kms from Kakinada. The nearest town is Rajanagaram, just 8 kms located on the route between Rajahmundry to Kakinada via Rajanagaram
